Our Story

Retro Buck was born from a passion for authentic Italian cuisine and a desire to share the rich culinary traditions of our homeland in Kanya. Founded in 1985 by the Rossi family, our restaurant has been serving the community with love, tradition and exceptional food.

Every dish tells a story, crafted from recipes passed down through generations and prepared with the finest ingredients. Our chefs are dedicated to preserving the authenticity of Italian cooking while adding their own unique touch. At Retro Buck, we believe that food is not just about sustenance, but about bringing people together and creating memories that last a life time.
